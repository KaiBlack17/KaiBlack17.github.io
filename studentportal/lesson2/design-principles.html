Three Design Principles

Kailey Black

Proximity

National Geographic

National Geographic does an excellent job of using proximity to it's advantage, many things are happening on the site, but they are sure to put related information together so that the user is not confused.

Visual Heirarchy

Artbeads

Visual hierarchy is strongly implemented on Artbeads.com, they make sure to highlight where they want your eyes to travel using shapes, colors, and large fonts to catch your attention.

White Space

RedBubble

White space is very effectively used on this site, everything is nicely organized and not crowded out. As you scroll down the page many categories are displayed, all with padding in between.
