Featured Companies

Arbor Day Nursery

2767 W 12600 S

https://arbordaynursery.net/

If you're looking for trees this is the place to go. There is a high demand for landscaping among all the new construction projects and rising population, and the Arbor Day Nursery has provided well for those needs since 2005. If you need a little more green in your yard, be sure to check them out.

Batch Baked Goods

3693 W 13400 S, Suite E

https://batchbakedgoods.com/

Here's a good place to go if you like Crumbl cookies, but are looking for someplace new to try. Instead of just cookies, they have a variety of delicious baked goods such as cupcakes, muffins, cookies, and cinnamon buns. But they don't just carry the classic flavors, here you can discover flavors and combinations you've never tried in a dessert before. Think of this place the next time you're craving something sweet.

Berrett's Blossoms

2762 W 12600 S, Suite 4

https://www.berrettsblossoms.com/

Flowers are amazing for various occasions, and if you're looking for something better than can be found at a grocery store, here's the place to go. They have arrangements for every occasion and unique vases that will become permanent decorations in the home. But they have more than just flowers, Berrett's also carries succulents, which are becoming increasingly popular as houseplants and will last much longer than roses.
